Paris


Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its rich history, iconic landmarks, vibrant culture, and world-class cuisine. It is a city that blends timeless elegance with modern charm, offering unforgettable experiences for travelers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The symbol of Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to thousands of artworks, including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture with stunning stained glass.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Located atop Montmartre hill with panoramic city views.
  5. Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue lined with shops, cafes, and theaters.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Baguette – Classic French bread enjoyed daily by locals.
  • Crème brûlée – A creamy dessert topped with caramelized sugar.
  • Escargot – A traditional delicacy of cooked snails.
  • Macarons – Colorful, sweet meringue-based confections.
